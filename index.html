– Hello

I’m Yassine El Ghayani a passionate Digital Art Director & Visual Designer with several years of experience, starting from the concept up to the final result. I'm a branding, typography and design lover.

I’m based in London , currently working as Senior Creative at Phantom and judge at  FWA.
